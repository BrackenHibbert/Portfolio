Content

Who Am I

The home page of my website will give a summary of who I am. The purpose of the main page is to introduce myself to potential employers who may be interested in hiring me. I plan to give a short summary of my life and try my best to describe who I am and why I would ne good for any team. This is just the home page with the intent to give a general overview. The secondary pages I plan to add will include my work history and experience, and then the other will include more details about my personal skills and and knowledge.

Images for the Home page

Work Experience

This page will hold my life and work experiences that may be relevant to being hired by potential employers. For example I will add what jobs I held, when I had them, and skills I worked on or learned while having that job. That will be the main content of the page, it's sole purpose is to show potential employers that I have experience working and whether or not I may have experience in a certain subject or field they are looking for.

Images for the Page 2

Personal Skills and abilities

The intent of this page is to basically convince potential employers that I am the employee they want. This page is the place for me to list both my hard and soft skills, any education I have, and any certificates I may have. The other two pages are all informational about myself, where as this one is more about persuading potential employers. The goal of this is ultimately to show why I am a skilled employee that will help them and their company.
